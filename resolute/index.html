What is Resolute Gold Token?

Resolute Gold is a token offered by Poliprom S.A.C. One Resolute Gold token represents one gram of gold on a London Good Delivery (LBMA) gold bar. The fractionalisation of Resolute Gold tokens is possible up to two decimal places (i.e. in increments as small as 0.01 gram).

What is the benefit of having physical assets on a blockchain?

Owning physical assets on the blockchain provides you with the combined benefits of both physical and digital assets. As a Resolute Gold token holder you will be able to enjoy ownership of gold while avoiding drawbacks associated with physical gold, such as high storage costs and limited accessibility.

How can I buy Resolute Gold Token?

1. You contact us and specify the payment method, where to send the Resolute Gold tokens.
2. After receiving the money (fiat or cryptocurrency), we check the time of the transaction and the price of gold in manual mode and fix the sale price of the token.
3. After that, we send tokens to the buyer's wallet (+5% tokens more).

What is the minimum purchase amount?

The minimum purchasing amount is 1 Resolute Gold token.

How can I sell Resolute Gold token?

You can sell your tokens to the company in 3 calendar months after purchase, at a fixed price LBMA at the time of purchase of the token. The company undertakes to repurchase all tokens.

How can I redeem my Resolute tokens?

With 1000 tokens or more you can exchange them for physical gold. To do this, you need to contact us and discuss the details of the agreement, fill the KYC form and provide the necessary documents. After receiving the physical gold, the customer sends tokens to the company's wallet, after which the tokens are burned.

Does your company have all the necessary mining permits and other documents?

Yes. For wholesale buyers, we provide all the documents that confirm the legality of our activity.

Why do you actually sell physical gold at a price below market?

We accumulate money from private investors for the development of our company. It is even advantageous for us to make a bigger discount because the profitability of gold mining and sales is now very profitable.
