LOCATION OF PROCESSING PLANT: LA OROYA, JUNIN, PERU

The processing plant is located in the Oroya region, near the village of Paccha.
The main line of operation of the factory is Polymetallic ores (Lead, Zinc, Copper). The factory is currently modernized (11/2023) to install additionally a gold Cyanidation line.
The Poliprom company INVESTS in the extraction of minerals in the region and throughout the country (Peru).



About Company

Poliprom S.A.C. is a Peruvian Mining Company. Specialization of the Company: Mining and Processing of precious and rare-earth Minerals (Gold, Silver, Zinc, Lead, Copper, Aluminium, etc).
